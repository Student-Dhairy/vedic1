Neem Tree

The Neem tree, native to the Indian subcontinent, is renowned for its powerful medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Almost every part of the Neem tree—its leaves, bark, seeds, and oil—contains beneficial compounds that promote health and healing.

Tulsi, also known as the "Queen of Herbs," is revered in Ayurvedic medicine for its numerous health benefits and spiritual significance. Native to the Indian subcontinent, Tulsi is a small, aromatic plant with green or purple leaves, slightly toothed at the edges. It typically grows 1-2 feet tall and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.
