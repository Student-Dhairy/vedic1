Tulsi

Tulsi, also known as the "Queen of Herbs," is revered in Ayurvedic medicine for its numerous health benefits and spiritual significance. Native to the Indian subcontinent, Tulsi is a small, aromatic plant with green or purple leaves, slightly toothed at the edges.
